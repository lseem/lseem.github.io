PhD student in geometric analysis supervised by Ben Sharp at the University of Leeds. Currently interested in stability of immersed CMC surfaces and minimal surfaces.

Talks

  • Leeds Pure PGR Seminar. 19 Feb 2024. “A Talk Wherein I Tell You as Much as I Can About the Isoperimetric Problem in a Fixed Amount of Time.”
  • Reading group: analytical aspects of harmonic maps. 17 Nov 2023. “ε-Regularity of weakly stationary harmonic maps II”. Hardy-BMO duality and Hölder continuity of weakly stationary harmonic maps with a Morrey growth condition.
  • Reading group: analytical aspects of harmonic maps. 03 Nov 2023. “ε-Regularity of weakly stationary harmonic maps I”. Hardy spaces and choice of Coulomb frame.
  • Reading group: analytical aspects of harmonic maps. 06 Oct 2023. I (being the first speaker) give an introduction to this topic.
  • Leeds Maths PGR Conference 2023. 15 Jun 2023. “Blowing bubbles in curved ambient spaces.” I present the results of my work in the first year of my PhD to a general mathematical audience.
  • Reading group: stability of minimal hypersurfaces. 12 May 2023. I present on the paper of N. Smale [1].
  • Warwick Maths Society Talks. 07 Dec 2021. “Is the Critical Catenoid Alone? Finding Friends of Minimal Surfaces using PDEs.” I present the results of my summer 2020 research project to other undergraduates.
